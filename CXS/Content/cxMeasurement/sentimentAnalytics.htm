Sentiment Analytics

  • What Is It?
  • Why Use It?
  • Target Audience
  • Who Uses It?
  • Included, Add-on, and Complementary Products & Measures
  • General Information
  • Important Links
  • Competitive Landscape

Sentiment Analytics takes open-ended comments from a ForeSee® customer experience measurement and processes them through a natural language processor to visually and conveniently reveal critical topics, keywords, and sentiment. This capability works in tandem with the predictive and quantified measurement system relied upon by ForeSee® clients. Users have instant access to Sentiment Analytics within their ForeSee® portal, providing immediate views of aggregate and respondent-level insights that drive more depth behind key satisfaction drivers impacting customer experiences.

Why Use It?

This natively integrated capability allows brands to deeply understand and act upon the intensity of the customer experience in the context of a predictive and prioritized framework. Sentiment Analytics benefits organizations by:

  • Simplifying the task of quantifying open-ended comments.
  • Enabling deeper segment analysis by joining text-based insights to quantified metrics.
  • Comparing overall sentiment to changes in customer experience programs over time.
  • Surfacing potential language to marketing and communication teams who benefit from knowing how topics and keywords resonate with targeted communities and customer groups.
  • Equipping Research or Customer Insights teams with visual data justifying the awareness among other functional teams when certain topics or keywords warrant attention.

Target Audience

Research / CX Analysts / CI Teams

Relevant Concerns:

  • Detecting opportunities or risks buried within verbatims.
  • Interpreting comments in the context of other reliable risk indicators.
  • Lengthy time spent reading or coding open-ends.
  • Accessing text analysis results.

How does Sentiment Analytics benefit them?

  • Dashboards conveniently visualize the nature of customer conversations and their sentiment.
  • Comments can be examined for identified segments already known to be helpful or harmful to a client's future.
  • Categories are created for them - keywords and topics are extracted and quantified based on their frequency and sentiment.
  • It's built right into AAP.

Customer Service Teams

Relevant Concerns:

  • Improving customer services.
  • Missing certain pieces of customer feedback.

How does Sentiment Analytics benefit them?

  • Negative keywords, topics, or full comments can be shared based on their overall sentiment. This can prevent potential customer loss/defections.

Marketing

Relevant Concerns:

  • Creating relevant and targeted communications.

How does Sentiment Analytics benefit them?

  • Highlighted language from a segment-level analysis can inform future copy writing or bring up concepts relevant to the studied segment (e.g., highly positive keywords tied to a product-shopping segment might warrant future use in advertising or on product description pages).

Complementary Touchpoints

Products Included In Base Contract

  • Requires a cxMeasure with an open-end question and AAP access.
  • Allows processing of up to 300,000 open-ends per month. Pricing is for 1.5 million API calls per month - but each verbatim requires five API calls.

Add-On Products to Consider

Additional blocks of 300,000 open-ends per month. Pricing is for 1.5 million API calls per month - but each verbatim requires five API calls.

Majority of clients do not need this.

General Information

Key Capabilities

  • Filter-driven Sentiment Analytics (using the filtering mechanisms within AAP).
  • Question-driven Sentiment Analytics (tying the data back to cxMeasurement Metrics).
  • Word cloud keyword visualization.
  • Focusing and managing content (keyword and topic extraction).

Configurations

  • Product Applications: Can be utilized on an open-end comments collected through cxMeasure products (including legacy Feedback). Not available on new Feedback at this time.
  • Contract Scope: If a client purchases Sentiment Analytics - it is applicable to all of their cxMeasures and all of their assigned portal users.
  • Portal Access: Only available within the AAP at this time.
  • Language: Only available on U.S. English comments at this time. We do not offer translation services for open-ends.
  • Hosted Code: Does not require hosted code or code at all. Can be utilized on any cxMeasure OE data populated to AAP.

Deployment

  • Client must have a signed contract to allow for Sentiment Analytics access to be granted.
  • Assigned CS contact must submit a ticket to DOT to have access assigned to the client’s user(s).
  • Two levels of users are available - Administrative and Viewer.
    • Administrative access allows for: 1) determination of which open-end questions can be run using Sentiment Analytics and 2) viewing of usage metering.

ForeSee® employees can be granted access to client’s Sentiment Analytics - but caution should be used around Foresee verses client usage and the volume of OE’s they have.
